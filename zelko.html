What We Do

Zelko is the energy and commodity trading division of ACE CAPITAL LIMITED, specializing in oil & gas services, international commodity trading, and energy market operations. We provide comprehensive solutions across the entire energy value chain.

Our experienced team of energy professionals and traders leverage deep market knowledge, advanced analytical capabilities, and strategic partnerships to deliver superior results in energy trading, market analysis, and risk management.

We serve energy producers, refiners, distributors, and end-users worldwide, providing tailored solutions that optimize their energy procurement, trading strategies, and market positioning in the global energy marketplace.
