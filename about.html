About ACE CAPITAL LIMITED

Building excellence in financial services since inception

Our History

ACE CAPITAL LIMITED was established with a vision to create a comprehensive financial services group that would serve diverse industries across global markets. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a respected conglomerate with expertise spanning finance, asset management, logistics, and energy sectors.

Over the years, we have built strategic partnerships and expanded our operations to meet the evolving needs of our clients. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has positioned us as a leader in the financial services industry, with a reputation for delivering reliable and effective solutions.

Today, ACE CAPITAL LIMITED operates through four main subsidiaries, each specializing in their respective domains while maintaining the highest standards of professional service and ethical business practices.

2010
Company founded with focus on financial services
2015
Expansion into asset management and logistics
2018
Establishment of energy and trading divisions
2025
Continued growth and international expansion
